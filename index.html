Tracker Boss

Tracker Boss

With the bug tracker application, teams can easily log and track bugs, assign them to specific developers, and monitor their progress. The application incorporates a role-based system, allowing administrators, project managers, developers, and submitters to have different levels of access and responsibilities within the bug tracking process. Project managers, developers, and submitters only have access to their assigned projects and/or tickets.

The bug tracker enables users to prioritize bugs based on severity levels and assign them to appropriate developers. It allows for the seamless addition of bug-related information, such as bug descriptions, attachments, and comments, facilitating effective collaboration and communication among team members.

To ensure efficient bug resolution, I have implemented status tracking, allowing team members to stay updated on the progress and resolution of each bug. When tickets are completed, they can be archived, and restored at any time.

Address Almanac

Address Almanac

This application provides a comprehensive solution for managing and organizing contact information effectively. With the address book application, users can easily store, retrieve, and update contact details in a user-friendly interface. The application incorporates CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations, allowing users to add new contacts, view existing ones, edit information, and remove entries as needed.

To enhance usability, I have implemented search and filtering functionalities. Users can quickly locate specific contacts by searching for names, phone numbers, or other relevant details. Additionally, filtering options based on categories or tags enable users to sort and organize their contacts based on personal preferences.

Security is a top priority, and I have ensured that the application follows best practices in data protection. User information is securely stored in a backend database, and authentication measures are implemented to restrict unauthorized access. This ensures the privacy and confidentiality of sensitive contact details.
